Are you looking to get involved?

Join us in bridging the digital divide. Whether you give your time, skills, equipment, or financial support, you're helping put working computers into the hands of people who need them.

Ways You Can Help

Computers 4 People in Need is an all-volunteer organization and is always looking for passionate individuals to join us in our mission. There are many ways to support our work. Explore the options below and choose what fits you or your organization best.

Volunteer Your Time

Help with refurbishing computers, basic tech support, intake events, and workshop days. No advanced experience required—just a willingness to learn and help.

  • Refurbish and test donated computers
  • Prepare systems for pickup days
  • Assist clients with basic setup
Sign Up to Volunteer

Internship Opportunities

Gain hands-on experience in IT, repair, documentation, and community outreach while making a real impact in people’s lives.

  • Ideal for students and emerging tech professionals
  • Learn hardware, software, and support skills
  • Build experience for your resume or portfolio
Ask About Internships

Donate Devices

We accept working or repairable laptops, desktops, and monitors. Your older equipment can become someone else’s lifeline to school, work, and services.

  • Laptops & desktops (Windows, Mac, or Chromebook)
  • Monitors, keyboards, and mice
  • We securely wipe data from all donated devices
View Device Donation Guidelines

Support With a Donation

Financial gifts help cover the cost of parts, repairs, software, and support so every device that goes out is reliable and ready to use by someone in need.

  • Help fund parts to upgrade and repair donated equipment
  • Help expand our reach in the community
  • Your donation is tax-deductible
Donate Online
