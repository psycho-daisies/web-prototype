What Having a Computer Helps You Do

A computer isn’t just a device—it’s a way to stay connected, learn, work, and manage everyday life.

Connect with Friends & Family

Make video calls, send messages, and stay in touch with loved ones across town or across the world.

Virtual Appointments

Meet with doctors, case workers, and services online without needing to travel.

Browse the Web

Look up information, find resources, and explore opportunities that aren’t available offline.

School & Learning

Attend online classes, finish homework, access learning tools, and build new skills.

Pay Bills & Manage Life

Pay bills, manage benefits, fill out forms, and keep important documents organized.

Work & Job Searching

Search and apply for jobs, attend interviews, and work remotely.
